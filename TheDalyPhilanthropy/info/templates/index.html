About The Daly Philanthropy

The Daly Philanthropy is a social service project aimed at providing education to underprivileged students in science, mathematics, computer science, and arts. Our project aims to bridge the educational gap that exists in our society by offering quality education and practical skills to children who are unable to afford it. We believe that every child has the right to education, regardless of their financial background. Through our project, we aim to empower students by providing them with the necessary tools and resources to achieve their academic goals and succeed in their personal and professional lives. We have been inviting groups of roughly 20 students to our school, The Daly College and giving them the chance to witness the wide variety of opportunities the school offers. Our team of dedicated and experienced volunteers work tirelessly to create a safe and nurturing environment for our students to learn and grow. We understand that education goes beyond textbooks, which is why we also offer co-curricular activities like drawing and theatre.Â  We believe in the power of education to transform lives and communities. By investing in the education of underprivileged students, we hope to create a more equitable and just society where everyone has the opportunity to succeed. We welcome your support in our efforts to make a positive difference in the lives of these students.

Our aim

Our social service project promotes in-school education by inviting groups of 20 students to our school, The Daly College. We teach reading, writing, speaking, maths, science, and co-curricular activities like theatre to enhance their learning experience. Join us in making a difference in education today.
